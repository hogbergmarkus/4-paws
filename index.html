About us

First of all, we would never euthanize an animal unless it was medically necessary.


All the work that takes place here is carried out by volunteers, with a strong commitment towards the well-being of our animals.


Adoption

All applications to adopt an animal are carefully reviewed and home visits, both before and after adoption, are mandatory. Fill out the form that you find in in the application menu att the top of the page, to request the adoption of one of our animals.


Relocation

If you want our help to relocate your cat or dog, fill out our application, that you can find at the top of the page in the the navigation bar. NOTE that we will first look att what you have done yourself, to find a new home for your pet. If nothing, or not enough has been done, we will help guide you. If ultimately you can not find a home for your pet, we will take them in and continue the search.
